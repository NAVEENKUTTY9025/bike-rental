How It Works

Renting a bike with GrowwBike is quick and easy. Follow these simple steps to start your ride.

1

Choose Your Bike

Browse our selection of premium bikes online or visit one of our rental stations. Select the bike that matches your needs and preferences.

2

Book Online

Select your rental dates and duration. Complete the quick online booking form with your details. Choose pickup location and any accessories.

3

Pick Up & Ride

Visit our rental station at your selected time. Our staff will provide a safety briefing and helmet. Sign the rental agreement and start your adventure!

4

Return & Review

Return the bike to any of our stations. Share your experience and review our service. Get discounts on your next rental!
