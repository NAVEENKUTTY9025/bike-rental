Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our bike rental service.

What do I need to rent a bike?

You'll need a valid government-issued photo ID (driver's license, passport, or national ID) and a credit/debit card for the security deposit. For electric bikes, you must be at least 18 years old.

How does the security deposit work?

We place a hold on your card for the security deposit amount (varies by bike type). This hold is released immediately upon return of the bike in good condition, typically within 3-5 business days depending on your bank.

Can I extend my rental period?

Yes, you can extend your rental through our website or mobile app, or by contacting our support team. Extensions are subject to bike availability and may be charged at the current rate.

What happens if the bike gets damaged?

Minor wear and tear is expected and covered. For significant damage due to negligence or accidents, repair costs will be deducted from your security deposit. We recommend purchasing our damage waiver for full coverage.

Do you offer group or corporate discounts?

Yes! We offer special rates for groups of 5+ bikes and corporate accounts. Contact our sales team at corporate@GrowwBike.com for customized pricing and packages.
