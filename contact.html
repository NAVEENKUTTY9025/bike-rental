Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and support.

How do I rent a bike?

You can rent a bike through our website, mobile app, or visit any of our rental stations. Simply choose your bike, select rental duration, and complete the booking process.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay/Google Pay.

Are helmets included?

Yes, every rental includes a complimentary helmet. We also offer locks, lights, and child seats for an additional fee.

What is your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 24 hours before your rental starts. Cancellations within 24 hours are subject to a 50% fee.

What if my bike breaks down?

We provide 24/7 roadside assistance. Call our emergency support line, and we'll send assistance or arrange a replacement bike.

Can I return to a different location?

Absolutely! You can pick up from one station and return to any of our locations across the city at no extra cost.
