Discover Our Journey

Our Journey: From a Simple Idea to City-Wide Mobility

GrowwBike was born from a simple observation: cities are becoming more congested, while people crave freedom and flexibility in their daily commute. In 2018, our founders started with just 15 bicycles and a vision to make urban cycling accessible to everyone.

What began as a small bike-sharing initiative has grown into a comprehensive urban mobility solution. Today, we operate multiple stations across the city, offering a diverse fleet of premium bicycles for every type of rider.

Our mission extends beyond just renting bikes. We're committed to promoting sustainable transportation, healthy lifestyles, and community connections through the joy of cycling.

How We Operate

Our commitment to quality, safety, and customer satisfaction drives every aspect of our business.

1

Bike Selection & Maintenance

We carefully select each bike in our fleet based on durability, performance, and rider comfort. Every bicycle undergoes a 12-point safety check before and after each rental, with regular professional maintenance to ensure optimal performance.

2

Customer Safety First

We provide complimentary helmets with every rental and offer optional safety gear. Our team conducts quick safety briefings for first-time riders and provides city cycling maps with recommended safe routes.

3

Sustainable Operations

Our rental stations are powered by renewable energy sources. We use eco-friendly cleaning products and participate in city-wide recycling programs. For every 100 rentals, we plant a tree in partnership with local environmental organizations.

4

Community Engagement

We host monthly community cycling events, partner with local schools for cycling safety programs, and offer special rates for non-profit organizations. Our "Bike to Work" program helps reduce urban congestion during peak hours.
