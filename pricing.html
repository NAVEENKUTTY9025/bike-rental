Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our rental process, policies, and services.

What documents are required for bike rental?

You need a valid driving license (for the bike category), original ID proof (Aadhar Card, Passport, or Voter ID), and two passport-size photographs. For international tourists, a valid international driving permit and passport are required.

What is the security deposit and refund policy?

Security deposit varies based on the bike model (₹1500 to ₹5000). The deposit is fully refundable within 24 hours after bike return, provided there are no damages or traffic violations. Refunds are processed via the original payment method.

Can I extend my rental period?

Yes, you can extend your rental period. Please inform us at least 6 hours before your scheduled return time. Extension charges will be calculated based on the hourly/daily rate of your current plan. Extensions are subject to bike availability.

What happens in case of breakdown or accident?

All our Gallery are insured against accidents and theft. In case of breakdown, we provide 24/7 roadside assistance. For accidents, immediately contact our support team and file a police report if required. Please refer to our terms for detailed coverage information.

Do you offer delivery and pickup service?

Yes, we offer doorstep delivery and pickup within city limits for an additional fee of ₹200. For airport/railway station deliveries, the charge is ₹300. Delivery must be Booked at least 4 hours in advance. Free pickup and drop available at our rental stations.

Are there any age restrictions for renting?

Yes, you must be at least 18 years old with a valid driving license to rent our Gallery. For premium Gallery (above 250cc), the minimum age is 21 years. International renters must meet their home country's minimum age requirement plus have a valid IDP.
